## 5. Taylor / Maclaurin
- Exponential: $e^x = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{x^n}{n!}$
- Sine: $\sin x = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^n x^{2n+1}}{(2n+1)!}$
- Cosine: $\cos x = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^n x^{2n}}{(2n)!}$
## 6. Fourier
- Fourier series: $f(x) = \frac{a_0}{2} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\left(a_n\cos nx + b_n\sin nx\right)$
- Cosine coefficient: $a_n = \frac{1}{\pi}\int_{-\pi}^{\pi} f(x)\cos nx\, dx$
- Sine coefficient: $b_n = \frac{1}{\pi}\int_{-\pi}^{\pi} f(x)\sin nx\, dx$
## 7. Binomial
- Binomial theorem: $(a+b)^n = \sum_{k=0}^{n}\binom{n}{k}a^{n-k}b^k$
- Binomial coefficient: $\binom{n}{k} = \frac{n!}{k!(n-k)!}$
- Binomial expansion: $(1+x)^n = \sum_{k=0}^{n}\binom{n}{k}x^k$
