## 1. Arithmetic
- $a_n = a_1 + (n-1)d$ arith_nth_term
- $S_n = \frac{n}{2}(a_1 + a_n)$ arith_sum_of_n_terms
- $S_n = \frac{n}{2}[2a_1 + (n-1)d]$ sum_expended

## 2. Geometric
- $a_n = a_1 r^{n-1}$ geo_nth_term
- $S_n = a_1 \frac{1-r^n}{1-r}$ geo_sum_n_terms
- $S_\infty = \frac{a_1}{1-r}, \quad |r|<1$ geo_infinity_sum
